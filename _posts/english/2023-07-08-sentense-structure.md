## Sentence Structures

### **1형식.** `Subject` + `Verb`

### **2형식.** `Subject` + `Verb` + `Complement`

### **3형식.** `Subject` + `Verb` + `Object`

### **4형식.** `Subject` + `Verb` + `Indirect Object` + `Direct Object`

### **5형식.** `Subject` + `Verb` + `Object` + `Object Complement`
